# Marketing-Campaign-Analysis
This project presents a Power BI dashboard developed to analyze marketing campaign performance across different locations, channels, devices and seasonal campaigns. The dashboard highlights key performance indicators such as conversions, ROI, engagement rate, ad spend and profit, allowing stakeholders to make informed, data driven decisions.

## Project Overview
The dashboard provides insights into marketing activities by enabling users to filter the report by:
- City or Location
- Marketing Channel
- Date Range

The goal is to evaluate which campaigns and channels deliver the best results in terms of profit, engagement and ROI.

## Key Metrics
- Total Conversions: 1.73M  
- Total Profit: $2M  
- Average ROI: 593.8K%  
- Total Ad Spend: $163.3K  
- Average Engagement Rate: 1162%

## Dashboard Insights

### Click Through Rate by Device
- Desktop users show
- Desktop CTR: 7261.28%
- Mobile CTR: 4957.32%

### Spend and Profit by Campaign
- Fall campaign generated the highest profit at $0.67M.
- Summer and Spring campaigns produced moderate but positive returns.

### ROI by Campaign
- Summer campaign delivered the highest ROI at 2933%.
- Spring and Fall campaigns followed at 1812% and 1065%.

### Engagement Rate by Campaign
- Fall campaign recorded the highest engagement share at 69.59%.
- Spring and Summer campaigns showed consistent but lower engagement.
